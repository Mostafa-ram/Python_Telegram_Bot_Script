##### Telegram Chatbot Password Manager #####

you can add-select-delete-update user informations (userName-Password-id).
the Bot has (5) Commands [/start, /insert, /select, /update, /delete]

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
1- /start 
to start the conversation with the bot.

2- /insert
to insert your inf .. just write> /insert [id] [name] [password]

3- /select 
-you can select all users .. just write> /select
-you can select specific user .. just write> /select [_id]

4- /upadte
you can update your inf .. just write> /update [_id] [new_name] [new_password]

5- /delete
you can delete specific user .. just write> /delete [_id]
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

### important notes

*(_id) means that automatically generated id in db.
*you can get your telegram api_id and api_hash from this link> https://my.telegram.org/auth
